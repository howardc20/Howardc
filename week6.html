Week 6 | Vacuum Forming and Lamination- Skateboards

The Assignment: 1. Decorate your skateboard using the laser, vinyl cutter or vinyl printer. 2. Laminate your Skateboard using Titebond III glue. 3.Vacuum bag your skateboard. 5.Take pictures of your skateboard when you are finished and add pictures of both the front and back and describe the process to create the skateboard on your webpage.

The Process

I have a long board that I occasionly ride at home, so I decided that I would make a longboard because I do not know how to ride a normal skatebaord. With Charlie, I glued the pieces together and wedged them between two wooden planks to stick together over night. I came back the followng day and my board had stuck together well and I was now ready to add some flair to the wood. I took tape and masked a zig-zag pattern onto my board. Then, I took Cherry Red spray paint and covered the front side of the board. I think the result is really cool. For the bottom side, I took my sticker with my name on it and attached it to the wood, which was a good use of the existing materials I had.
